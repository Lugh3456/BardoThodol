# Bardo Thodol — The Tibetan Book of the Dead

A static, English-language mini site presenting the Bardo Thodol (中阴得度), the Nyingma terma on death, the intermediate state (bardo), and rebirth, attributed to the terma tradition of Padmasambhava as revealed by Karma Lingpa.

Spoke 9 of [The Dharma Gate](https://lugh3456.github.io/DharmaGate/) portal.

## Structure

11 sections following the traditional three-Bardo division:

1. Introduction
2. Chikhai Bardo (the moment of death)
3–8. Chonyid Bardo (the bardo of karmic visions — peaceful deities days 1–6, the Knowledge-Holders, and the wrathful deities)
9–10. Sidpa Bardo (the bardo of seeking rebirth — judgment of karma, signs of rebirth)
11. Conclusion (modern relevance)

Each section page includes the full text, line-by-line commentary, a "🔊 Listen" text-to-speech button, and a summary.
